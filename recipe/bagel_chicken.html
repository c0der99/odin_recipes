2-Ingredient Everything Bagel Chicken Wings

Description

These 2-ingredient air fryer everything bagel chicken wings are my take on easy, non-spicy wings. Perfectly seasoned, crispy on the outside, tender and juicy on the inside, and you really only need 2 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken party wings (wingettes or drumettes)
  • 2 tablespoons everything bagel seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 390 degrees F (199 degrees C).
  2. Place wings in a large bowl. Sprinkle everything seasoning over the top. Using your hands, mix well until wings are evenly coated.
  3. Place wings in the basket of the air fryer, skin side up. Cook for 20 minutes. Shake and cook until crisp, about 3 minutes more.
