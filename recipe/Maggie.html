Maggie Masala

Description

Simple white flour based noodles

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cup water
  • Maggie masala
  • two noodle cakes

Steps

  1. Pour 1.5 cup drinking water in a cooking vessel
  2. immerse th two noodle cakes in water when boiled
  3. add maggie masala
  4. cook for 5 minutes
  5. Serve hot
